HOW THE PROGRAM WORKS

1

We set up a heated pretzel station. You own nothing.

We place a plug-in countertop display that keeps pretzels hot and ready to serve — plus all serving materials (napkins, bags, tongs). Zero cost, zero installation, zero risk.

2

You order pretzels from us. Your staff serves them.

Pretzels go from our kitchen to the station in minutes. No chef. No prep. No training. Your existing staff handles it exactly like serving any other packaged food.

3

Your guests pay $7–8. You keep all of it.
